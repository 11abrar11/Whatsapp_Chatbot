# Case Studies

## Creative Marketing Collateral for DFW Airport Parking

### Client
A certified U.S.-based marketing agency responsible for delivering promotional assets for DFW Airport Parking, one of the largest and busiest airport parking facilities in the United States.

### The Ask
The client approached PP5 Media Solutions to develop a series of highly engaging marketing collateral for DFW Airport Parking. The requirement included:
- Static and animated digital banners
- Creative assets for seasonal promotions, loyalty campaigns, and limited-time offers
- Designs aligned with DFW’s brand guidelines while maintaining high visual impact and consistency across channels
- Fast turnaround to meet time-sensitive campaign launches

### Our Solution
PP5 Media Solutions assembled a dedicated creative team of designers and motion graphic specialists to deliver a seamless experience. Our approach included:
- **Brand Deep-Dive:** Studied DFW’s branding guidelines and past campaigns to ensure alignment with tone, color palette, and messaging.
- **Design Execution:** Produced a suite of static banners, GIFs, and animated HTML5 banners optimized for performance across web and mobile platforms.
- **Agile Collaboration:** Maintained a 24x5 communication channel with the U.S. agency team via email, Skype, and shared cloud folders to enable real-time feedback and revisions.
- **Quality Assurance:** Conducted thorough design and animation quality checks to ensure pixel-perfect output and cross-platform compatibility.

### The Impact
- Successfully delivered over 400+ creative assets across multiple campaign cycles
- The animated banners achieved a significant boost in click-through rates (CTR) compared to previous static creatives
- Client praised the turnaround time, visual creativity, and quality of execution, leading to an ongoing retainership
- Strengthened the U.S. agency’s delivery capabilities and helped them retain and grow their engagement with DFW Airport Parking

## Empowering Social Impact Through Creative Campaigns for Goodwill Dallas

### Client Overview
Goodwill Dallas, founded in 1923, is the first Goodwill organization in Texas. As a pioneering nonprofit, it is committed to helping individuals especially adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) and others facing barriers to employment achieve economic independence through job placement and workforce development.
Operating through a network of donated goods retail stores and a comprehensive workforce development program.

### The Ask
Goodwill Dallas approached PP5 Media Solutions with the need for creative design and production support to elevate the visibility and impact of its awareness and fundraising campaigns. Their specific goals included:
- Designing print and banner materials for community outreach, events, and retail stores
- Creating professional, emotionally resonant videos to support donation drives and program visibility
- Aligning all creative assets with their mission-driven messaging and brand identity
- Enhancing engagement with both donors and community stakeholders

### The Solution
PP5 Media Solutions responded with a holistic creative strategy designed to drive engagement, build trust, and visually communicate Goodwill Dallas's impact.
Our key deliverables included:
- **Print Design:** Developed flyers, brochures, posters, and other collateral for both in-store and community use—featuring strong visuals, clear calls-to-action, and inclusive messaging.
- **Banner Design:** Created eye-catching static and digital banners for events, donation centers, and social campaigns, ensuring consistency across platforms and physical spaces.
- **Video Production:** Produced professional, high-quality videos that highlighted personal success stories, donor impact, and community initiatives. These videos served as key tools in donor outreach and digital marketing efforts.
- **Collaborative Workflow:** Maintained close coordination with the Goodwill Dallas team to ensure each asset reflected their mission, values, and strategic objectives.

### The Impact
The creative assets delivered by PP5 Media Solutions played a critical role in boosting Goodwill Dallas’s community engagement and fundraising results:
- **Increased Donations:** Campaign videos and materials significantly improved donor response, helping raise substantial contributions during targeted fundraising periods.
- **Improved Visibility:** Enhanced branding and storytelling through print and video assets helped reinforce Goodwill’s presence and credibility within the local community.
- **Strengthened Mission Messaging:** Professional designs communicated the organization's impact clearly and emotionally, aligning well with their long-term vision for 2030.
- **Ongoing Relationship:** The success of the initial campaigns laid the foundation for continued collaboration and creative support.
